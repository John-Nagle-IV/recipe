Almond Flour Bread

Ingredients

  • 200g almond flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 20g psyllium husk
  • 4 medium Egg (beaten)
  • 50g coconut oil (melted)
  • 125ml warm water
  • butter (grease loaf tin)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (177 degrees C).
  2. Grease a loaf tin with butter
  3. Mix dry ingredients together in a large bowl.
  4. Stir in wet ingredients (eggs, coconut oil, and warm water).
  5. Pour into greased loat tin
  6. Bake for about 30 minutes, until firm and golden.
  7. Cool
